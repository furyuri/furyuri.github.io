Had a successful time image streaming this morning around 4:00 am - 6:00 am.
Clear (still weak, but very present and noticable) and abundant images, voices, memories, etc. 

Some learnings and reminders:
- **Do it while close to sleep**: before bed, between sleep (after 1st sleep), after waking. Being in this broader liminal state (perhaps Theta Brain Wave heavy), makes the imagery surface more easily and vivid.
- Much easier to *not* speak/describe the sensory thoughts out loud, but to instead **describe using mental/inner dialogue**. Softer, quieter and easier to drop into the "flow" state.
- **There is a "flow" / almost trance state** that is reached, which aligns with the idea of it being a "stream". And as the flow state is reached, the more readily the images appear, fluidly and abundantly like a widening creek that flows into a river. It's like day-dreaming, perhaps?
- ***Remember: verbalizing***, even though it doesn't feel like it, **actually does help** the flow/imagery to increase and really get going/keep going. The key is to just use inner-dialogue, and not feel the need to speak out loud. The verbal mind needs to be soft and barely present.
- **Spend less time describing autogogic imagery**, and just do typical phantasia/sensory thought description. Emotions, songs in the head, shapes, themes, describe them all. 
- **No need to use a recording device.** It's another thing to worry about and thus break you out of the flow/non-verbal mode.
